I am an MSc student majoring in Data Science at [The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen)](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en). Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://me.sjtu.edu.cn/).

## **Research Interests**

My primary research interests revolve around **LLM Safety and Agents**. Very recently, I have been actively exploring **Multi-modal Large Language Models (MLLMs)**.

In the past, I engaged in research on Trustworthy AI (particularly algorithmic fairness) as well as other LLM-related topics. I remain open to exploring various other research directions.
