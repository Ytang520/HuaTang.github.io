I'm a MSc. student majoring in Data Science at [The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) (CUHKSZ)](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en). I previously obtained my B.Eng. from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://me.sjtu.edu.cn/).

## **Research Interests**

My research interests lie in **LLM Safety and Agents**. Recently, I'm working on **MLLM (Multi-modal Large Language Models)**.

I previously studied Trustworthy AI (esp. algorithmic fairness), as well as other LLM-related topics. I'm open to exploring other research directions.

My CV can be downloaded here: [CV](files/CV_Frank(Hua)_Tang.pdf)
